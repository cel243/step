About Me

I'm Caroline and this is my portfolio! A bit about me: I'm currently a rising Junior at Cornell's College of Engineering, majoring in Computer Science. On campus, I sing in the University Chorus and I also work as a TA for CS 2800: Discrete Math, one of my favorite courses I've taken at Cornell. My interests outside CS include Music and Linguistics, but I've known I wanted to study Computer Science ever since I received my first programmable calculator in middle school! Since beginning my degree, I've taken many fascinating classes in many areas of the field, including Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Smartphone Computing Tech, Functional Programming, Data Structures, and more! I've loved them all, but I have a special place in my heart for theory classes like Algorithms. It's hard to say whether I have a favorite field within CS, since I'm still exploring and learning about new career paths and options, but I've had a great time so far working with robotics, data management, machine learning, and software engineering, and I can't wait to see what more I'll learn during this internship!
